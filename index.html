About Me

Hi, I'm Fatima Ahmad. I'm a front-end developer who enjoys turning ideas into clean, functional websites. I focus on writing maintainable code using SCSS and following the BEM methodology to keep projects organized and scalable. I have strong skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and I'm comfortable using Git and VSCode. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills, especially in making websites accessible and responsive across all devices. With a background in computer engineering, I combine technical knowledge with creativity to build useful digital experiences. I have contributed to several projects, including an interactive portfolio website and a UI design system. My goal is to become a professional front-end developer who delivers high-quality user experiences. When I’m not coding, I love exploring design trends and experimenting with UI/UX concepts to make interfaces that users enjoy. I'm also a team player who values clear communication and effective time management.
